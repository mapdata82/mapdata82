The cities of the past had grown without the Internet,
and have been adapting to the growing prevalence of Internet, in the recent years.

At mapdata, we believe that the cities and towns of the future, can do much better,
while incorporating Internet as a basic service, from the the beginning.

We believe that this will help us with a more democratic access to planning and opportunities.

And we are quite keen on designing and building the information_service_infrastructure,
that will be a part of the organic loop, involved in the making of cities, for more than 100 years into the future.

The future should involve better planned investments.
